Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup Sun Dried Tomato
  • 1 cup Heavy Cream
  • 1 cup Chicken Broth
  • 2 tablespoons Unsalted Butter
  • 1/4 Grated Parmesan Cheese
  • 3 cloves Minced Garlic
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat.
  2. Add the minced garlic and cook until fragrant.
  3. Whisk in the chicken broth and cook. 1-2 min.
  4. Stir in the heavy cream, sun--dried tomato, and Parmesan. Cook for 1 min.
  5. Season the sauce with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Serve sauce over cooked pasta or your choice of meat.
